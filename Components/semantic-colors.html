Semantic colors

Semantic tokens describe color by its role in the interface (background, text, mud-border, state). They map to primitive colors and stay consistent across light and dark themes — use them in product UI instead of referencing primitive values directly.

Click any token pill to copy its name. The middle column shows the value used in the light theme; the right column shows the value used in the dark theme.
